**Purpose:** Reduce unnecessary waiting time and retry attempts by scheduling as close as possible to the exact 7-day mark, with just 3 seconds to compensate for infrastructure latency.

**Output:** Updated scheduling constants and logic using seconds-based advance instead of minutes-based.
</objective>

**Current Implementation:**
- `DEFAULT_ADVANCE_MINUTES = 5` (5 minutes before 7-day mark)
- `RETRY_INTERVAL_SECONDS = 30` (retry every 30 seconds)
- `MAX_RETRY_ATTEMPTS = 10` (total retry window: 5 minutes)
- `calculateBookingTime()` accepts `advanceMinutes` parameter

**Why This Was Wrong:**
The previous quick task (260411-blm) assumed we needed to arrive 5 minutes early and retry. However, testing revealed that the booking window opens **exactly** at the 7-day mark, not earlier. The 5-minute advance just caused unnecessary waiting and retries.

**New Strategy:**
- Schedule exactly 3 seconds before the 7-day mark (minimal Inngest latency compensation)
- Reduce retry interval to 3 seconds (if window still closed, retry quickly)
- Keep MAX_RETRY_ATTEMPTS = 10 (total fallback window: 30 seconds)
</context>
